How It Works?

  1. Oxyhydrogen gas or HHO is produced by our machine, which is then introduced into the air intake of the engine.

  2. Vehicle is set kept in idling for 30-40minutes.

  3. The gas is combusted with fuel and air mixture, this reaction changes carbon deposits into small particles and carbon returns to a gaseous state, which is then expelled through the exhaust system.
